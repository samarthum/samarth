Lost and Found

My latest single 'Lost and Found' is about finding what you love and losing yourself in it. It's a song dedicated to music, arts, people, conversations and everything that allows humans to forget they exist for a few moments and just get lost in what they're doing. 'Lost and Found' has been featured on RADAR India on Spotify and has 7000+ streams.

Jamboora

My first official release, this song is about resilience, and moving forward in the face of adversity by recognising that you don't always have to win or succeed. Sometimes it's necessary to let go of all expectations, and just be. Sometimes it's okay to just exist, to just be Human.

Creeper

This was my attempt at creating an eerie and creepy soundtrack. One of my favorite things about this composition is the drum part, which is an interesting combination of simple time signatures to create a more complex sounding part.

Khoj - A WCMT Cover

A cover of one of When Chai Met Toast's most popular track. Transcribed all the parts by ear and produced it from scratch. The cover was shared by WCMT themselves and was very well received.
