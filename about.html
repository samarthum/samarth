I am an independent singer-songwriter from Bangalore. Inspired by artists such as John Mayer, Coldplay, Steven Wilson and AR Rahman, I have been an active part of the Bangalore Indie music circuit since 2017.

My latest single, Lost and Found, was released in October to a widely positive response.

I'm also an integral part of the Bangalore based A Capella group No Treble, who have been ranked in the top 10 A Capella groups of India. No Treble also represented India in a global A Capella concert which was held in Lincoln Centre, New York in 2018.

In my free time, I like to read non-fiction books about topics ranging from Economics to Science to Philosophy. My next single will be releasing in February, 2021.
